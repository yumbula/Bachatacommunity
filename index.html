Our Story

More than a bar —
a living stage

Bachata Community was born from a simple belief: that the best nights are the ones where music does the talking. Nestled in the heart of Tbilisi, we've built a space that transforms every evening into something unforgettable.

From intimate DJ sessions to full live band performances, our carefully curated programme honours the Latin tradition while embracing Tbilisi's own electric energy. The room breathes with every beat.

We are a bar, a restaurant, and a stage — all in one. Come for dinner, stay for the dance.
